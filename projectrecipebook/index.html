Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4
Parmesan cheese 100g
Black pepper to taste

Details

  • Serves: 4
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes

Instructions

1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to the package instructions.

2. In a frying pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.

3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper.

4. Drain the spaghetti and mix it with pancetta and its fat.

5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg mixture until creamy.

6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese on top.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Cocoa powder 3/4 cup
Sugar 2 cups
Baking powder 1 1/2 tsp
Milk 1 cup
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup
Eggs 2

Details

  • Serves: 8
  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 30 minutes

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

3. In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.

4. Add milk, oil, and eggs; mix until smooth.

5. Divide the batter between the prepared pans.

6. Bake for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

7. Let cool before frosting.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup, grated
Caesar dressing 1/2 cup

Details

  • Serves: 4
  • Prep time: 10 minutes

Instructions

1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.

2. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.

3. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss well.

4. Serve immediately.
